Classic Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a rich, hearty Italian dish made with layers of pasta, savory bolognese sauce, creamy béchamel, and melty mozzarella cheese. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings, it’s a comforting meal that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef (for Bolognese sauce)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil, garlic, onion, herbs (like basil and oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna pasta sheets according to the package instructions, then set them aside.
  3. Prepare the Bolognese sauce: In a large pan, sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, then add the ground beef. Cook until browned. Add tomato sauce and simmer. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. For the béchamel sauce: In a saucepan, melt butter, then stir in flour to form a roux. Gradually add milk while whisking continuously until smooth and thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: In a baking dish, layer pasta, followed by Bolognese sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers and finish with a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
