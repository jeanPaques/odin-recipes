Sweet Crêpes Recipe

Description

Sweet crêpes are thin French pancakes perfect for breakfast or dessert. Fill them with your favorite toppings like sugar, Nutella, fruit, or jam for a delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • 250g flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 500ml milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (plus extra for the pan)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, melted butter, and salt until smooth.
  2. Let the batter rest for at least 30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Heat a lightly buttered non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan and swirl to coat evenly.
  5. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden.
  6. Repeat until all batter is used.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings: sugar, jam, chocolate, or fruit.
