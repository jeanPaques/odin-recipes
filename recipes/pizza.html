Pizza Margherita Recipe

Description

Pizza Margherita is a classic Italian pizza made with a thin crust, fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. It's simple, flavorful, and a staple of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Tomato sauce (or crushed San Marzano tomatoes)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) or the highest setting possible.
  2. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella evenly across the pizza.
  5. Bake the pizza for 7-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Slice and serve hot!
